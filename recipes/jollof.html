JOLLOF

Jollof rice is s nigerian delicacy packed with so much nutrients and flavor

INGREDIENTS

  • Rice
  • Tomatoe paste
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Chicken stock
  • salt
  • Fresh pepper and tomatoe blended mix
  • onions
  • vegetable oil
  • Chicken
  • Ginger & garlic cloves

STEPS

  1. Wash the peppers, onion, and tomatoes.
  2. Blend the chopped ingredients together.
  3. Rinse the rice in water until it runs clear.
  4. Heat the vegetable oil over medium heat.
  5. Chop 1 onion and saute it in the oil for 4 minutes.
  6. Cook the tomato paste for 5 minutes.
  7. Add in the pepper puree and spices.
  8. Add the rice, chicken stock, water, and salt.
  9. Cover and cook the rice for 20 min.
  10. Simmer the rice for an additional 10-20 min.
  11. Fluff and serve the jollof rice. Serve the jollof with fried plantains, meat, or salad.
